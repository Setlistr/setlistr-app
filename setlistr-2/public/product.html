02 — Identify

Composition-level matching, not recording fingerprinting.

Most audio detection tools match against studio recordings. They fail with live music — different key, different tempo, different arrangement.

Setlistr matches on lyrics, melodic patterns, and structural signals. A live cover of "Fast Car" is correctly identified as Tracy Chapman's composition regardless of who's playing it.

The Full Workflow

Everything that happens
before royalties arrive.

1

Open app before the show

Search or add your venue. Setlistr pulls venue info, capacity, and PRO territory automatically.

2

Play your set

Setlistr listens passively. No audio stored. Composition matching runs in real time.

3

Review the detected setlist

After the show, review and approve the auto-detected setlist. Edit any low-confidence matches. Add anything missed.

4

Select PROs and submit

Choose which PROs to report to and which territories apply. One tap submits to all simultaneously in the correct format.

5

Track status and collect

Every submission is logged in your dashboard with status tracking. Royalties typically arrive 9–18 months after submission.
